Our Services

Comprehensive Pool Care for Las Vegas Homeowners

Weekly Service: During each visit, we brush walls and steps, check and adjust chemicals, net the pool, vacuum when necessary, empty skimmer and pump baskets, and visually inspect all equipment.

Maintenance: Filter cleanings quarterly, acid/chlorine washes every 3-4 years, and tile cleanings as desired. Proper chemistry kills germs and bacteria, prevents algae growth, and protects your equipment.

Repairs: As a licensed contractor, we handle all pool equipment repairs with knowledge, strong work ethic, and cost-consciousness. Financing is available through Enhancify for costly repairs.

Consulting: First-time pool owner or need expert advice? We provide professional guidance on pool care, equipment, and maintenance schedules tailored to the Las Vegas climate.

We don't require contracts because we believe we have to earn your business with every visit. Our owner, John Butler, is NSPF Certified, licensed by the Nevada Contractor Board (Lic. #0082744), and fully insured and bonded. Every team member shares our commitment to honesty, quality workmanship, and going above and beyond for our customers.

We accept debit, ACH, Zelle, and credit card payments.

Our Team & Training

Behind every great pool service is a great team. Our 12 certified team members are always in uniform, easy to identify, and trained to deliver exceptional results. Every Clean Living technician is certified by the National Swimming Pool Foundation as a Certified Pool Operator (CPO).

We invest in ongoing training so our crew stays current on the latest pool care techniques, safety standards, and equipment technology. Our staff managers hold CPO certifications, and we emphasize emergency preparedness, teamwork, and professionalism in everything we do.

We are available 24 hours a day, seven days a week, year round. Whether it's your weekly service visit or an after-hours emergency, call us at (702) 539-3839 and we'll take care of it.
